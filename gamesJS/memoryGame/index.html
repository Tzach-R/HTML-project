Rules of the Game:


  1. Your main goal is to match pairs of cards until all cards on the board are matched.
  2. Flip over 2 cards at a time, try and find matching pairs.
  3. If they don't match the card will flip over again, face down.
  4. If they match they will stay facing up-right.
  5. The game continues until all pairs are matched, once youv'e matched them all you won!
